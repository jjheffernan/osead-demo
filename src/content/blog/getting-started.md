## Start with the rhythm of your week

The best beach weeks have space for the unplanned parts: a late breakfast, a
walk before dinner, a quiet hour on the deck. Begin with the people joining you
and the pace you want to keep.

## Choose the setting before the bedroom count

Oceanfront mornings, a sheltered bay, or a short walk to town each create a
different kind of stay. Use the listing details to narrow the setting first,
then compare the homes that fit.

## Keep one day open

Leave room for the local things that do not fit an itinerary: a market stop,
an empty stretch of sand, or a restaurant recommendation from a neighbor.

## Ask early

If you are deciding between homes, send an inquiry with your dates, group size,
and non-negotiables. O-sea-D can help you understand the fit, but an inquiry
never confirms a reservation.
